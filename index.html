Knoxville CocoaHeads

Welcome to the CocoaHeads of Knoxville, TN!

CocoaHeads is a group devoted to discussion of Apple's Cocoa and Cocoa Touch Frameworks for programming on OS X (Mac) and iOS (iPhone, iPad). During monthly meetings, members offer tutorials, present their projects, share app ideas, and provide advice to other programmers. Whether you are an experienced developer or just getting started, Knoxville CocoaHeads is a great way to stay current with the latest technologies and improve your programming skills. For more information about the history of CocoaHeads, visit the organization's global website at http://cocoaheads.org.

CLLocation

Monthly meetings are every 3rd Tuesday from 7-9 pm at the Knoxville Entrepreneur Center (KEC) located between Subway and Soccer Taco in Market Square of downtown Knoxville. Parking decks are free after 6 pm.
