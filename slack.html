Join CocoaHeads on Slack

If you would like to join the CocoaHeads community on Slack, please submit your information in the form below. Your email address is required for the invite but your name and which CocoaHeads group you are involved with is optional. You should receive your invite in a day or two.

Each CocoaHeads chapter is encouraged to create a channel for their local group. For example, the "knoxcocoa" channel is for members of the Knoxville CocoaHeads.
